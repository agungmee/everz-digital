Drone Usage

Guests who bring their own drone for usage within the national park are required to purchase a drone ticket at the National Park Office at Labuan Bajo 3 days prior of the trip. The cost of the ticket is 2 million rupiahs so please do prepare enough cash.

Cancellation Policy

  1. If the harbour master does not give clearance for departure, guests can choose full refund or reschedule.
  2. If it is raining heavily the morning of departure, we will wait 1 hour max. If it still rains heavily, reschedule or full refund. If only drizzling, we will still depart.
  3. If your flight is cancelled/rescheduled by the airlines (proof needed) and no substitute flight is given, guests are allowed full refund or reschedule.
  4. If guests are Covid positive, they are allowed full refund/reschedule (proof of PCR test needed).
  5. Other than the above, no cancellation/rescheduling is allowed 2 days prior to departure. Full payments will still be collected.

Booking Procedures

  1. All bookings via WhatsApp chat/call.
  2. We provide pricing details and discuss your needs.
  3. After confirmation, we issue a booking code (keep it safe).
  4. Booking code is valid until 7 days before the pick-up date.
  5. Make payment or deposit before the booking code expires.
  6. If expired, booking is not guaranteed.
  7. Rebooking is required if the code is invalid.
  8. Admin will send payment details and invoice via WhatsApp.
  9. Prices exclude government taxes.

Refund Policy

  • Less than 7 days before pick-up date: no refund.
