Batur Sunrise and Natural Hot Spring

Join us on the Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking and Natural Hot Springs experience and enjoy the stunning sunrise from 1,717 meters above sea level, overlooking Bali's most active volcano and thousands of temple islands. While soaking in the breathtaking view, we'll serve you a cup of coffee or chocolate, complemented with a light breakfast including banana sandwich, hard-boiled egg, seasonal fruits, and chocolate bars.

During the Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking and Natural Hot Springs, you can see steam rising from several hot spots on the active volcano summit, with temperatures exceeding 100C. Here, we cook eggs and bananas for your breakfast using the volcanic heat. Simply dig a small hole in the sand, place the eggs and bananas inside, cover them with grass, wait 10-15 minutes, and enjoy your unique volcanic-cooked breakfast.

The Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking and Natural Hot Springs covers a total distance of about 9 km up and down, making it suitable for those with an average fitness level. After the hike, you can relax in the Batur natural hot springs and relieve tight muscles from the trek, making this combined package a perfect balance of adventure and relaxation.

As part of the Mount Batur Sunrise Trekking and Natural Hot Springs experience, the hot spring water comes from rocks with temperatures around 35-37C. This water travels through volcanic activity under Mount Batur, creating natural hot springs. It is also believed to have benefits for skin health. On the way back to your hotel, you will visit the hot springs, or optionally a coffee plantation, before returning home safely.
