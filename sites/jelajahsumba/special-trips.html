Sumba Island Experience (4D/3N)

Day 1: Arrival, village visit, and sunset

Arrive at Tambolaka (TMC) Airport and meet your guide. After lunch at a local restaurant, visit Bondo Kapumbu Village, a hidden traditional village in South West Sumba. Continue to Ketewel Beach to meet local salt farmers and see how salt is made traditionally. End the day at Lendongara Hill for sunset, then check in to your hotel.

Meals: Lunch

Accommodation: Mario Beach Hotel (Superior Sea View)

Day 2: Cashew farm, ricefields, and Watubela Beach

After breakfast, visit Talasi to see the cashew farm and production process, and taste fresh cashew milk. Continue to Waikacura for a ricefield trek with river views and meet local farmers along the way. Lunch at a local restaurant in West Sumba, then relax at the hidden Watubela Beach before returning to the hotel.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: Rua Beach Hotel (Deluxe Room - ROH)

Day 3: Cooking experience and Lapopu Waterfall

After breakfast, visit a traditional village in West Sumba for a cultural welcome. Join locals to cook Sumba's traditional foods and enjoy lunch together, followed by coffee or tea. Continue to Lapopu Waterfall for a short hike and bamboo bridge crossing to the natural pool. Return to the hotel afterward.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: Rua Beach Resort (Deluxe Room - ROH)

Day 4: Praijing Village and departure

After breakfast and check-out, travel toward East Sumba with a stop at Praijing Village, a hilltop traditional village surrounded by lush greenery. Meet locals and explore the village before continuing to Tambolaka (TMC) Airport for departure.

Meals: Breakfast
