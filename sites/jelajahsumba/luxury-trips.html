Lelewatu Resort Getaway (5D/4N)

Day 1: Arrival and resort check-in

Arrive at Tambolaka (TMC) Airport and meet your guide. Transfer to Lelewatu Resort for check-in. The resort sits on a dramatic clifftop setting overlooking the Indian Ocean, surrounded by lush rainforest.

Meals: -

Accommodation: Lelewatu Resort

Day 2: Ricefields, village views, and Lapopu Waterfall

Start the day with a short trek through Waikacura rice fields, where local farmers work among river views and a small waterfall. Continue to Praijing Village, a hilltop traditional village with panoramic overlooks and beautiful natural surroundings.

In the afternoon, visit Lapopu Waterfall. A short hike and bamboo bridge lead to the falls and a refreshing pool for a swim if you wish.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: Lelewatu Resort

Day 3: Market, villages, and Watubela Beach

Visit a traditional market to see local products and daily life, then continue to Tarung/Waitabar Village. After lunch, explore Wanokaka and its traditional villages: Waigali, Praigoli, and Waiwuang. End the day at Watubela Beach, a hidden gem on the West Sumba coast.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: Lelewatu Resort

Day 4: Resort staycation

Free day to relax and enjoy the facilities at Lelewatu Resort.

Meals: Breakfast

Accommodation: Lelewatu Resort

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, transfer from the resort to TMC Airport for your onward flight.

Meals: Breakfast

Start: TMC Airport (Tambolaka) | Finish: TMC Airport (Tambolaka)

Sanubari Beach Resort Escape (5D/4N)

Day 1: Arrival and resort check-in

Arrive at Tambolaka (TMC) Airport and transfer to The Sanubari Beach Resort for check-in. The resort sits within a lush reserve on the ancestral lands of the Marapu people, surrounded by white-sand beaches and world-class surf.

Meals: -

Accommodation: The Sanubari Beach Resort

Day 2: Ricefields, village views, and Lapopu Waterfall

Start the day with a short trek through Waikacura rice fields, with river views and a small waterfall at the end. Continue to Praijing Village, a hilltop traditional village with panoramic viewpoints and lush surroundings.

In the afternoon, visit Lapopu Waterfall. A short hike and bamboo bridge lead you to the falls and a natural pool for a refreshing dip.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: The Sanubari Beach Resort

Day 3: Market, villages, and Watubela Beach

Visit a traditional market to meet locals and see handmade products. Continue to Tarung/Waitabar Village, then explore Wanokaka and its traditional villages: Waigali, Praigoli, and Waiwuang. End the day at Watubela Beach.
