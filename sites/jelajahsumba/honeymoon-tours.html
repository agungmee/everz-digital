Sumba Honeymoon Adventure (4D/3N)

Day 1: Arrival, culture, and cashew & coffee experience

Arrive at Tambolaka (TMC) Airport and meet your driver and guide. After lunch, visit Rumah Budaya Sumba to learn about the island's heritage and see traditional artifacts and Marapu stone carvings.

Continue to Talasi Estate Factory to see cashew and coffee production, explore the farm, and taste cashew milk and weejewa coffee. Check in to your resort.

Meals: Lunch

Accommodation: Cap Karoso Resort - Beach Suite

Day 2: Weekuri, Mandorak, Ratenggaro, and Pero sunset

Start at Mandorak Beach for coastal views and rocky cliffs, then visit Weekuri Lake for a swim in crystal-clear saltwater. Continue to Ratenggaro Village to see the iconic high-roofed houses and coastal setting. End the day with sunset at Pero Beach.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box

Accommodation: Cap Karoso Resort - Beach Suite

Day 3: A day of love

Enjoy a relaxed day at the resort, followed by a 90-minute couple's spa treatment. In the evening, savor a romantic dinner prepared by the beach.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Accommodation: Cap Karoso Resort - Beach Suite

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to TMC Airport based on your flight schedule. End of trip.

Meals: Breakfast

Sumba Love Getaway (4D/3N)

Day 1: Arrival in East Sumba

Arrive at Waingapu (WGP) Airport and meet your driver and guide. Enjoy sunset at Walakiri Beach, then check in to your resort and have dinner.

Meals: Dinner

Accommodation: Kandora Luxury Villa (Deluxe Ocean View)

Day 2: Ikat culture, waterfall, and savannah coast

Visit Prailu Late King Village to explore ikat weaving traditions and megalithic tombs. Continue to Tanggedu Waterfall, then trek through savannah landscapes before ending at Puru Kambera Beach.

Lunch at a local restaurant and dinner at the resort.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Kandora Luxury Villa (Deluxe Ocean View)

Day 3: A day of love

Enjoy a relaxing resort day and a 90-minute couple's spa treatment. Lunch is served at the villa, followed by an intimate romantic dinner by the beach.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Kandora Luxury Villa (Deluxe Ocean View)
