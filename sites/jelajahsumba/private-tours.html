Explore Sumba Island (3D/2N)

Day 1: Arrival, culture, and sunset views

Arrive at Tambolaka (TMC) Airport and meet your driver and guide. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant, then visit Rumah Budaya Sumba, a traditional Sumbanese-style cultural house with Marapu stone carvings and collections of ikat, wood carvings, jewelry, and daily artifacts.

Continue to Talasi Estate Factory to see how local cashew and coffee are produced, explore the farm, and taste cashew milk and weejewa coffee. Check in to your hotel, then head to Lendongara Hill for a calm sunset with panoramic hill and ocean views. Return to the hotel after sunset.

Meals: Lunch

Accommodation options: Maringi Eco Resort (Bamboo Pavilion) | Mario Beach Hotel (Superior Sea View) | Ella Hotel (Deluxe Room)

Day 2: Lagoons, beaches, and villages

After breakfast, travel to the Kodi area. Swim or relax at the crystal-clear Weekuri Lake and explore the surrounding rocks and reef with ocean views. Visit Mandorak Beach, framed by dramatic rock ridges and clear water. A lunch box is provided as there are no restaurants nearby.

Continue to Ratenggaro Village to see the iconic high-roofed houses and learn about traditional community life. End the day at Pero Beach for sunset, then return to the hotel.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box

Accommodation options: Maringi Eco Resort (Bamboo Pavilion) | Mario Beach Hotel (Superior Sea View) | Ella Hotel (Deluxe Room)

Day 3: Local market and departure

After breakfast and check-out, visit a local market to see how residents sell their products and interact with the community. Transfer to Tambolaka Airport for your onward flight. End of trip.

Meals: Breakfast

Start and finish point

TMC Airport (Tambolaka)

After breakfast and check-out, travel to East Sumba with scenic stops along the way. Visit Praijing Village, a hilltop traditional village with lush surroundings and panoramic views. Continue to Anakalang in Central Sumba to see the megalithic tomb of the late king in Pasunga Village. Lunch boxes are provided.

Check in at your East Sumba hotel, then head to Walakiri Beach for sunset and explore the photogenic mangrove trees in the water. Return to the hotel after sunset. Lunch is served at a local restaurant.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation options: Morinda Villa (Deluxe Hill View Room) | Kambaniru Beach Resort (Deluxe Garden Room) | Padadita Beach Hotel (Superior Room)

Day 5: Ikat Tenun village and departure

After breakfast and check-out, visit a village known for Ikat Tenun, the traditional Sumbanese fabric. Try wearing the local costume and take a family photo. Transfer to WGP Airport (Waingapu) for your onward flight. End of trip.

Meals: Breakfast
