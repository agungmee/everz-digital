Festival Pasola Kodi (4D/3N)

Day 1: Arrival, villages, and coastal highlights

Arrive at Tambolaka (TMC) Airport and meet your guide. After lunch, visit Ratenggaro Village in the Kodi area to see the iconic high-roofed houses and coastal setting. Continue to Weekuri Lake for a swim and time around the rocks and reef with ocean views. End the day at Mandorak Beach, then check in to your hotel.

Meals: Lunch

Accommodation options: Mario Beach Hotel (Deluxe Bungalow) | Mario Beach Hotel (Superior Sea View) | Ella Hotel (Deluxe Room)

Day 2: Pasola Kodi, ricefields, and Praijing Village

After breakfast and check-out, return to Kodi for the Pasola Festival, where horsemen from opposing groups compete in ceremonial spear battles. The festival is held once a year in February or March, on a date chosen by the Rato priest to honor ancestors and ask for fertility. Lunch boxes are provided. After the festival ends around noon, continue to Waikacura for a short ricefield trek with river views and a small waterfall, then visit Praijing Village, a hilltop traditional village with panoramic views.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation options: Rua Beach Hotel (Deluxe Room - ROH) | Morikka / Manandang Hotel (Standard Room) | La Casa De Luna (Standard Room)

Day 3: Wairinding Hill, Ikat Tenun, and Walakiri sunset

After breakfast and check-out, travel to East Sumba with a stop at Wairinding Hill. A lunch box is provided. Visit an Ikat Tenun village to see traditional weaving and try on local costumes for photos. Check in, then enjoy sunset at Walakiri Beach with its iconic mangroves.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box

Accommodation options: Morinda Villa (Deluxe Hill View) | Kambaniru Beach Hotel (Deluxe Garden View) | Padadita Hotel (Superior Room)

Day 4: Departure

Breakfast and free time until transfer to WGP Airport (Waingapu). End of trip.

Meals: Breakfast

Festival Pasola Lamboya (4D/3N)

Day 1: Arrival, villages, and coastal highlights

Continue to Weekuri Lake for a swim and time around the rocks and reef with ocean views. End the day at Mandorak Beach, then check in to your hotel.

Meals: Lunch

Accommodation options: Mario Beach Hotel (Deluxe Bungalow) | Mario Beach Hotel (Superior Sea View) | Ella Hotel (Deluxe Room)

Day 2: Waikacura ricefields and Lamboya village

After breakfast and check-out, explore West Sumba with a visit to Waikacura for a ricefield walk and river views. Continue to Lamboya Village to see preparations for the Pasola festival. If time allows, relax at a nearby beach before checking in to your hotel in West Sumba.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation options: Rua Beach Hotel (Deluxe Room - ROH) | Morikka / Manandang Hotel (Standard Room) | La Casa De Luna (Standard Room)

Day 3: Pasola Lamboya and Lapopu Waterfall

Early morning transfer to Lamboya with breakfast boxes. Witness Pasola Lamboya and the traditional horsemen ceremonies. After the festival, have lunch at a local restaurant, then visit Lapopu Waterfall with a short hike and bamboo bridge crossing. Return to your hotel.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box

Accommodation options: Morinda Villa (Deluxe Hill View) | Kambaniru Beach Hotel (Deluxe Garden View) | Padadita Hotel (Superior Room)

Breakfast and free time until transfer to TMC Airport. End of trip.
