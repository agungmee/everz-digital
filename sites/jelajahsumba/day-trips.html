Day Trips in Sumba

Full-day journeys blending cultural heritage, coastal beauty, and natural escapes across West and Southwest Sumba.

Sumba Heritage & Coastal Bliss: Megalithic Villages, Weekuri Lake & Mandorak Sunset
A journey through ancient culture and stunning seascapes in Southwest Sumba

Uncover the living history and breathtaking landscapes of Southwest Sumba. Begin in Ratenggaro and Toda Villages, where towering traditional houses and ancient megalithic tombs tell stories of Sumba’s past. Meet the friendly locals, learn about their customs, and witness daily life in these timeless communities. Continue to the turquoise waters of Weekuri Lake for a refreshing swim, then relax on the pristine shores of Mandorak Beach as the sun sets over the Indian Ocean. A perfect balance of culture, adventure, and serenity.

West Sumba Wonders: Waikelo Sawah, Villages & Watubela Beach
Discover the Hidden Treasures of West Sumba on an Unforgettable Full-Day Adventure!

Embark on a scenic journey through the lush landscapes and timeless traditions of West Sumba. Begin your adventure with a tranquil trek at Waikelo Sawah, passing rice terraces, rivers, and hidden waterfalls. Enjoy a local lunch before exploring the cultural heart of Wanokaka, where villages like Waigali, Praigoli, and Waiwuang reveal Sumba’s living heritage. End the day at the serene Watubela Beach, a secluded gem perfect for relaxation and sunset views. A day of pure Sumbanese beauty and connection with nature.

Cultural & Nature Escape: Praijing Village, Local Market & Lapopu Waterfall
Discover the Hidden Treasures of West Sumba on an Unforgettable Full-Day Adventure!

Discover the charm of Praijing Village with its panoramic hilltop views and traditional houses surrounded by lush nature. Visit a lively local market to meet the friendly Sumbanese people before continuing to the stunning Lapopu Waterfall, where you can enjoy a short hike, cross a bamboo bridge, and swim in the refreshing natural pool.

Sumba Heritage & Coastal Bliss

Full Day Tour: Traditional villages and coastal highlights

After breakfast, explore the Kodi area of South West Sumba. Visit Ratenggaro Village to see the iconic high-roofed houses and hear stories of Sumbanese heritage. Continue to Toda Village, another traditional settlement with megalithic tombs and warm local welcomes.

Enjoy scenic stops along the road to see village tombstones and free-roaming horses. Visit Weekuri Lake for a swim and time around the rocks and reef with ocean views. A lunch box is provided. End the day at Mandorak Beach, framed by two dramatic rock ridges and crystal-clear water, then return to your resort.

West Sumba Wonders

Full Day Tour: Ricefields, villages, and hidden beach

After breakfast, head to Waikelo Sawah for a scenic trek through rice fields, river views, a natural spring in a cave, and a small waterfall. Lunch is served at a local restaurant.

Continue to Wanokaka to explore traditional villages: Waigali, Praigoli, and Waiwuang. End the day at Watubela Beach, a secluded coastal spot in West Sumba.
