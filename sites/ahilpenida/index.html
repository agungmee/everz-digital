Important Notes

  • Flight consideration: at least 18 hours after a dive before flying.
  • Pool refresher is required if you have not dived in the last 12 months.
  • Pool session (non-certified) is done 1 day before at 15:00 or 16:00 at the office infinity pool.
  • Ocean dive meeting point: Ahiltourandtravel Shop, Toyapakeh Beach.
  • A 30% deposit is required before the pool session (transfer/PayPal).
  • Price excluded: conservation area ticket 100,000 per pax per day (cash).
  • Pool session transport not included.
  • Pick up/drop off within a 5 km radius of the dive center.
